üéØ AI Sandpit Use Case Workshop ‚Äî Facilitator Guide

Your Goal

Help participants identify 2-3 concrete AI use cases they could prototype in their Datacom AI Sandpit. Move them from vague interest ‚Üí specific, testable ideas they're excited to try.

‚è±Ô∏è 30-Minute Activity Run Sheet

Before

Your Slides, Quiz & Story ‚Äî Sets up the activity

Examples, context setting, loosening people up.

üé¨ Transition: "Now let's flip it ‚Äî instead of me talking at you, let's explore what's relevant to YOUR world. I've got some cards here..."
0-3

Card Spread & Quick Browse

  • "Grab 2-3 cards that spark something ‚Äî doesn't have to be your industry"
  • Keep it moving ‚Äî this is just to seed conversation
3-15

Share & Translate

  • "What caught your eye about this one?"
  • "What's the equivalent in YOUR world?"
  • Capture ideas on whiteboard as you go
15-25

Deep Dive: Pick 1-2 Ideas & Probe

  • Push for specifics: "How many? How often? What system?"
  • Find the champion: "Who would be the first user?"
  • Size the prize: "What would 30% faster mean for you?"
25-30

Commit & Close - Take Away Exercise

  • "Which ONE use case would you most like to see working?"
  • "What's the smallest version we could test in 2 weeks?"
  • Capture: who owns what, what data they'll provide
‚è∞ Tight on time? Cut Deep Dive to 5 mins and focus on ONE use case only. Better to go deep on one than shallow on three.

üö´ Common Objections & Responses

They Say... You Say...
"Our data isn't ready" "What's the smallest dataset you could test with? A sandpit lets you experiment without production-grade pipelines."
"We don't have the skills" "That's what the sandpit solves ‚Äî low-risk learning. What if we start with config, not code?"
"Leadership won't approve it" "What proof point would convince them? That's your sandpit goal."
"We tried AI and it failed" "What went wrong? (Listen.) Sandpit de-risks that ‚Äî small experiments, fast feedback."
"It's not a priority" "What IS the priority? Could AI accelerate that?"
"What about hallucinations?" "Which use cases are 'human in the loop' where that's manageable? Start there."

üé¨ Closing Questions to Drive Commitment

  • "Of everything we discussed, which ONE use case would you most like to see working?"
  • "What would a successful proof of concept look like for you?"
  • "Who else in your organisation needs to see this to believe it?"
  • "What's the smallest version we could test in the sandpit in 2 weeks?"
  • "What data would you need to provide to make this prototype real?"
